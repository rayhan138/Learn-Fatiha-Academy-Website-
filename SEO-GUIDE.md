## 🔍 COMPETITOR RESEARCH

### How to Research Competitors:

**1. Find Your Competitors:**
Search Google for your target keywords and note the top 10 results:
- "online quran classes for kids"
- "quran academy for children"
- "learn quran online"

**2. Analyze Their Websites:**
For each competitor, check:
- What keywords are they targeting? (Look at title, headings, content)
- How much content do they have? (Word count)
- What pages do they have? (About, Courses, Blog, etc.)
- Do they have a blog? How often do they post?
- What's their domain authority? (Use Moz or Ahrefs)

**3. Tools to Use:**
- **Free Tools:**
  - Google Keyword Planner (keyword research)
  - Ubersuggest (competitor analysis)
  - AnswerThePublic (content ideas)
  - Google Trends (trending keywords)
  
- **Paid Tools (Optional):**
  - Ahrefs (backlink analysis, keyword research)
  - SEMrush (competitor analysis)
  - Moz (domain authority, keyword tracking)

**4. What to Look For:**
- Keywords they rank for (steal their keywords!)
- Their backlink sources (get backlinks from same sites)
- Their content topics (create better content)
- Their social media strategy (learn from their posts)
