## 🎯 Your Goal
Find out what your competitors are doing and do it better!

---

## Step 2: Analyze Each Competitor

### For Each Website, Check:

**1. Their Homepage:**
- What's their main headline?
- What keywords do they use?
- What's their unique selling point?
- Do they have student videos?
- Do they show teacher profiles?
- Do they have testimonials?

**2. Their Content:**
- How many pages do they have?
- Do they have a blog? How many posts?
- How long are their blog posts? (aim for 800-1500 words)
- What topics do they write about?
- Do they have FAQ page?

**3. Their SEO:**
- View their page source (Right-click > View Page Source)
- Look at their `<title>` tag - what keywords do they use?
- Look at their meta description
- Do they have structured data (JSON-LD)?

**4. Their Social Media:**
- Do they have Facebook page? How many followers?
- Do they have Instagram? How often do they post?
- Do they have YouTube? How many videos?
- What type of content do they post?

**5. Their Pricing:**
- Do they show prices on website?
- What packages do they offer?
- Are they cheaper or more expensive than you?

## Step 3: Use Free SEO Tools

### Tool 1: Ubersuggest (Free)
**Link:** https://neilpatel.com/ubersuggest/

**How to Use:**
1. Go to Ubersuggest
2. Enter competitor's domain (e.g., "tarteelequran.com")
3. Click "Search"
4. You'll see:
   - Their monthly traffic
   - Top keywords they rank for
   - Their top pages
   - Their backlinks

**What to Do:**
- Steal their best keywords
- See which pages get most traffic
- Find out where they get backlinks from

### Tool 2: AnswerThePublic (Free)
**Link:** https://answerthepublic.com/

**How to Use:**
1. Enter keyword: "online quran classes"
2. Click "Search"
3. You'll see:
   - Questions people ask (e.g., "how to learn quran online")
   - Prepositions (e.g., "quran classes for kids")
   - Comparisons (e.g., "online vs offline quran classes")

**What to Do:**
- Use these questions as blog post titles
- Answer these questions on your website
- Create FAQ page with these questions

### Tool 3: Google Keyword Planner (Free)
**Link:** https://ads.google.com/home/tools/keyword-planner/

**How to Use:**
1. Sign in with Google account
2. Click "Discover new keywords"
3. Enter: "online quran classes"
4. You'll see:
   - Search volume (how many people search per month)
   - Competition (low, medium, high)
   - Related keywords

**What to Do:**
- Target keywords with high volume + low competition
- Use these keywords in your content

### Tool 4: Google Trends (Free)
**Link:** https://trends.google.com/

**How to Use:**
1. Enter keyword: "online quran classes"
2. See if it's trending up or down
3. Compare multiple keywords
4. See related queries

**What to Do:**
- Focus on trending keywords
- See seasonal trends (e.g., more searches during Ramadan)

## Step 4: Analyze Their Backlinks

### Where Do Competitors Get Backlinks?

**Check Manually:**
1. Search Google: `link:competitorwebsite.com`
2. See which websites link to them
3. Try to get backlinks from same websites

**Common Backlink Sources for Quran Academies:**
- Islamic directories (IslamicFinder, MuslimDirectory)
- Local mosque websites
- Islamic parenting blogs
- Education directories
- Social media profiles
- YouTube channel descriptions
- Forum signatures (Islamic forums)

|------------|-------------|----------|----------------|
| Competitor A | $50 | 4 classes/month | You offer more flexibility |

  - Better qualified teachers
  - More flexible schedule
  - Better student results (show videos!)
  - More personalized attention

## Step 8: Learn from Their Reviews

### Find Their Reviews:
1. Google their business name + "reviews"
2. Check Google Business Profile reviews
3. Check Facebook reviews
4. Check Trustpilot or other review sites

### What to Look For:

**Positive Reviews (What They Do Well):**
- "Great teachers" → Highlight your teachers too
- "Flexible schedule" → Emphasize your flexibility
- "Kids love the classes" → Show your student videos

**Negative Reviews (Their Weaknesses = Your Opportunity):**
- "Hard to schedule" → Make scheduling easy
- "Teachers not punctual" → Be always on time
- "No trial class" → Offer free trial
- "Expensive" → Offer competitive pricing
- "No refund policy" → Offer money-back guarantee

**Your Strategy:**
- Do what they do well, but better
- Fix what they do poorly
- Highlight these advantages on your website

Based on competitor research, highlight:

**Your Unique Selling Points:**
1. ✅ Certified, experienced teachers (show credentials)

6. ✅ Money-back guarantee (if not satisfied)

**Add These to Your Homepage:**

- "What makes us different..."
- "Why parents choose us..."
